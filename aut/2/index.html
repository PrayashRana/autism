About Autism

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental condition affecting communication, behavior, and social interaction. Each individual is unique.

Early support, understanding, and inclusive environments help individuals with autism thrive and reach their full potential.

Key Facts

Different Spectrum

Autism affects individuals differently.

Early Support Matters

Early intervention improves development.

Communication Differences

Speech and expression vary widely.

Sensory Sensitivity

Sounds and lights may feel overwhelming.

Routine Comfort

Structure provides safety and stability.

Unique Strengths

Many show exceptional talents.

Myths vs Facts

❌ Myths

Autism is a disease

They cannot learn

Vaccines cause autism

Autism only affects children

All autistic people are the same

Autistic individuals cannot communicate

Autism is caused by bad parenting

✅ Facts

Autism is a neurological condition

They feel emotions deeply

They learn differently

No scientific evidence supports this claim.

It is a lifelong condition.

Autism is a spectrum with diverse abilities.

Communication styles vary widely.

It is not caused by parenting.
